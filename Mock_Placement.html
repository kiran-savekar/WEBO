As the name suggests, this event refers to a ‘mock’ placement round for all the undergraduate

Guidelines for Competition

Rules and Regulations
          Round 1: General Aptitude
                           Time limit - 60 Minutes
                           Total Questions - 50
                            [No negative marking]
  
           Round 2: Baffle Round
                           Time limit - 30 Minutes
                           Total Questions - 25
                           [No negative marking]

           Round 3: Group Discussion
                           This round consisting of Group Discussion on current trends or technology.

            Round 4: Interview
                            In this round, participants will be challenged for personal interviews.

General Guidelines:-
    1] Additional rules and all other detailed information of competition will be disclosed on the spot.
    2] Any violation of rules will lead to disqualification.
    3] The decision of the judges shall be final and binding
